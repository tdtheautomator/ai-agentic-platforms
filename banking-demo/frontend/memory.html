About These Memory Stores:
In-Context: Active conversation turns (LLM context window). Limited to 10 turns. Cleared between runs. ❌ Does NOT survive restarts (RAM only).
Episodic: Persistent audit trail (SQLite database). Shows who did what, when, and the outcome. ✅ SURVIVES restarts (persisted to disk).
Semantic: Vector embeddings of banking rules, regulations, fraud signals, and past decisions (ChromaDB). Used for similarity search. ✅ SURVIVES restarts (persisted to disk).
Working: Fast cache layer (Redis) for deduplication and cross-agent state. TTL-bound keys (120s). ❌ Does NOT survive restarts (in-memory only).
